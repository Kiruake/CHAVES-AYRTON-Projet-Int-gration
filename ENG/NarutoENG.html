LE PHENOMENE NARUTO

完全な成功を

UN SUCCES TOTAL

Naruto is a shonen manga drawn by Masashi Kishimoto. His first appearance dates from 1999 in the Weekly Shonen Jump, Japanese magazine in which promising new manga appear, and ended in this same magazine in 2014, for a total of 72 volumes. Then was adapted into an animated series in 2002 by the Pierrot studio.

完全な成功を

250 million is the number of copies sold worldwide, otherwise Said Naruto is a staple in its kind, it is the 3rd in the world sales ranking. Its enormous success even gave birth to movies and video games that have done just as well, The Naruto franchise is a success from A to Z.

忘れられない冒険

UNE AVENTURE INOUBLIABLE

But what is Naruto? Naruto is the eponymous name of the protagonist with the blond hairstyle and eyes blue clothed in an orange jumpsuit that you must have seen once, no matter how old you are

Naruto is an orphan and had a terrible childhood. Locked up with a “demon” inside of him, who has in the past attacked his village. All Konoha hates and despises him, but he has a dream of becoming “Hokage” the head ninja of his village and thus prove to everyone his fair value and earn their respect. But the road will be long, accompanied by his friends, he will meet many more dangerous antagonists. others.

人生の哲学

UNE PHILOSOPHIE DE VIE

Through his adventures Naruto takes us from sadness to the joy, of hate to love and vice versa. One complete universe with a touch of humor, Naruto is a real life lesson in its entirety that whether by the stories of the characters or the course of the manga.
